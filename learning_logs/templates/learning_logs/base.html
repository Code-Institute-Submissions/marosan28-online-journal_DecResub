Taking notes, why?

Organizing your thoughts and understanding the material better Studies have shown that taking notes can help you remember the information you've learned for longer periods of time. Having notes to refer back to can be helpful when you need to review the material at a later date. Encouraging active learning: Taking notes requires you to be actively engaged with the material, rather than just passively listening or reading. This active learning can help you better understand and retain the information.
